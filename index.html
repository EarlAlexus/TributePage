Yuji Nishida

The left-handed Ace of Japan

Here's a timeline of Yuji Nishida's highlights (2018-2019):

  • January 2018 - Nishida made his V.League debut with JTEKT Stings
  • June 2018 - He was instrumental in Japan’s first win in 11 years against Italy, scoring 24 points on 21 spikes and three aces.
  • September 2018 - Scored 30 points in a match against Argentina at the 2018 FIVB Volleyball Men’s World Championship.
  • June 2019 - Shattered the VNL’s serving records when he struck 7 aces in a single game during a match against Canada.
